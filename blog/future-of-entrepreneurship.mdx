### What Changes

**Education**:
- Business schools teach Business-as-Code
- Accelerators focus on ICP definition, not pitch decks
- Coding bootcamps teach AI agent orchestration

**Funding**:
- Pre-seed rounds disappear (bootstrap instead)
- Seed rounds only for rapid acceleration
- Series A for proven $10M+ ARR startups

**Team Structure**:
- "CTO" title disappears (AI does engineering)
- "VP Marketing" becomes "Marketing Strategy" (AI does execution)
- Founders focus on: vision, strategy, key partnerships

**Competition**:
- Speed becomes primary advantage
- Iteration velocity > team size
- AI quality > human expertise (for most functions)

**Employment**:

- More bootstrapped millionaires

**Geography**:
- Location irrelevant (AI is global)
