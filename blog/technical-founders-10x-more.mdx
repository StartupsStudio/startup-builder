## The Mental Shift

### From Precious to Pragmatic

**Old mindset**:
- "This is my baby, my one shot"
- "I need to perfect every detail"
- "Failure means I failed"

**New mindset**:
- "This is one of many bets"
- "Ship fast, learn fast"
- "Failure means I learned what doesn't work"

### From Linear to Parallel

**Old approach**:
- Idea → Build → Launch → Scale
- 100% focus on one thing
- Serial processing

**New approach**:
- Ideas → Rapid launch → Parallel validation → Scale winners
- Portfolio management
- Parallel processing

### From Emotional to Analytical

**Old decisions**:
- "I believe in this"
- "I've invested too much to quit"
- "Just one more feature"

**New decisions**:
- "Data says this is/isn't working"
- "Sunk cost fallacy, cut losses"
- "Double down on what works"
